Air fryer baked potatoes

Description

A baked potato in the air fryer comes out beautifully crusty on the outside and light and fluffy on the inside,
just what a great baked potato should be! Serve them with your favorite toppings, like sour cream, butter, or chives.

Ingredients
  • 2 large russet potatoes, scrubbed
  • 1 tablespoon peanut oil
  • Â½ teaspoon coarse sea salt
Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Brush potatoes with peanut oil, sprinkle with salt, and place them in the air fryer basket.

  3. Cook potatoes until very tender when pierced with a fork, about 1 hour. Serve and enjoy!
